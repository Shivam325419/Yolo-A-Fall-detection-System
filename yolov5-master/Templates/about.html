About Us

Our Mission

Varishta Rakshak is dedicated to enhancing the lives of the elderly through innovative technology. Our mission is to create a world where seniors can maintain their independence while receiving the care and attention they need.

Our Vision

At Varishta Rakshak, we envision a future where every senior citizen lives with dignity and security. We aim to harness the power of artificial intelligence to ensure the safety and well-being of our elderly population, especially when they are alone.

Our Values

We prioritize safety, dignity, and independence. Our values guide us in providing reliable and compassionate care for the elderly.
